BUSINESS PROBLEM

A telecom company was losing 1 in 4 customers and needed to understand why — and predict which customers would leave next so the retention team could act before it's too late.

KEY FINDINGS
  • 26.5% overall churn rate — 1 in 4 customers leaving
  • Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate
  • Fiber optic customers churn significantly more than DSL
  • Churned customers pay $13.18 more per month on average
  • Customers who churn leave within the first few months
  • Total charges and tenure are the top churn drivers
APPROACH

Complete end-to-end data pipeline — raw data cleaning, EDA with 5 business charts, Logistic Regression model trained on 5,634 records and tested on 1,409 achieving 82% accuracy. Results presented in an interactive Power BI dashboard.

BUSINESS PROBLEM

Analyse electric vehicle registration data across the USA to understand EV adoption trends, manufacturer market share, geographic distribution and electric range performance.

KEY FINDINGS
  • Tesla dominates with 68,983 vehicles — nearly half of all EVs
